Case Study

Digital Strategy & Delivery: Renewables Energy Platform

Accelerating the energy transition for Shell Energy.

Overview

Shell Energy aimed to accelerate the global energy transition by providing accessible and reliable renewable energy solutions. The objective was to build a robust digital platform that connected consumers and businesses with sustainable energy products and services.

The Problem

The renewable energy market is complex and rapidly evolving. For a major energy provider like Shell, the challenge was to create a digital platform that could effectively communicate the value of renewable energy, simplify complex offerings, and facilitate seamless customer journeys from exploration to adoption. Existing digital touchpoints were often siloed, leading to a fragmented customer experience and a lack of clarity around renewable options.

The Solution

As Head of Design, I spearheaded the Digital Strategy & Delivery for Shell Energy's Renewables Energy Platform. My contributions spanned the entire product lifecycle, emphasizing UX design and strategic methodologies:

  • Vision & Roadmap Definition: Articulating a clear digital vision for Shell's renewable energy offerings, translating high-level business goals into a comprehensive, user-centric product roadmap. This involved extensive stakeholder management and alignment.
  • Ecosystem Mapping & Service Design: Understanding the diverse needs of residential, commercial, and industrial customers. We performed deep ecosystem mapping and applied service design principles to chart out the end-to-end digital and physical touchpoints for renewable energy products (e.g., solar, wind, EV charging) and services.
  • User-Centric Journey Design: Designing intuitive and transparent customer journeys for understanding, purchasing, and managing renewable energy subscriptions and solutions. This included detailed user journey mapping and iterative prototyping.
  • Agile Delivery Leadership & Design System Integration: Guiding cross-functional agile development teams (product, engineering, marketing) through continuous delivery cycles, ensuring a strong focus on user value and continuous improvement. We leveraged an existing design system to maintain brand consistency and accelerate development.
  • Data-Driven Decision Making: Implementing mechanisms for collecting and analyzing user data to inform design iterations and measure the impact of features, ensuring a truly data-driven design approach.

The Impact

The development of the Renewables Energy Platform positioned Shell Energy as a key player in the clean energy transition, delivering:

  • Accelerated Adoption of Renewables: A clearer, more accessible digital offering facilitated increased customer engagement and adoption of renewable energy products.
  • Enhanced Customer Trust & Transparency: The platform provided detailed information on energy sources and impact, fostering trust among environmentally conscious consumers.
  • Operational Efficiency: Streamlined digital processes reduced reliance on manual interventions for customer onboarding and service management.
  • Future-Proofed Growth: The scalable platform laid the foundation for Shell Energy to introduce new renewable products and services as the market evolves.
