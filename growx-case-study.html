Case Study

Fully Automated & Sustainable Vertical Farm

Designing digital controls for GROWx.

Overview

GROWx, a pioneering vertical farming company, sought to create the most efficient and sustainable food production system possible. This demanded a human-centered approach to designing the digital controls and monitoring systems for their fully automated vertical farm.

The Problem

Operating a fully automated vertical farm presents immense complexity. Managing thousands of data points related to plant growth, environmental conditions, and automation systems in a way that ensures both optimal yield and sustainability is a significant challenge. The risk of human error in such a precise environment is high, and a lack of intuitive control could negate the benefits of automation.

The Solution

I was instrumental in shaping the UX design and digital experience for GROWx's Fully Automated & Sustainable Vertical Farm, focusing on creating intuitive control interfaces and data visualization systems that allowed operators to manage this complex system with ease. My responsibilities included:

  • System Controls UX & UI Design: Designing user interfaces for monitoring and controlling all aspects of the farm, from irrigation and lighting to nutrient delivery and CO2 levels. This involved creating intuitive dashboards, alarm systems, and interactive controls to minimize cognitive load and prevent errors.
  • User Research & Workflow Analysis: Collaborating closely with horticultural experts and automation engineers through user research and workflow analysis to ensure the digital tools aligned perfectly with the operational procedures of the farm.
  • Data-Driven Optimization & Visualization: Translating scientific data on plant biology and environmental needs into actionable insights for the farm operators. This involved designing effective data visualizations that enabled real-time adjustments for optimal growth and resource efficiency.
  • Sustainability Tracking & Reporting: Integrating metrics and visualizations that showcased the farm's environmental impact, such as water usage, energy consumption, and carbon footprint. This not only reinforced GROWx's sustainability goals but also provided operators with insights for further optimization.
  • Prototyping & Iterative Design: Employing prototyping and iterative design cycles to test and refine the control systems, ensuring they were robust, user-friendly, and highly effective for precision agriculture.

The Impact

The intuitive digital control system was critical to the success of GROWx's vertical farm, leading to:

  • Maximized Yield & Quality: Precision control of environmental factors resulted in consistently higher yields and superior produce quality.
  • Unparalleled Resource Efficiency: The system enabled GROWx to achieve significant reductions in water and energy consumption, demonstrating the viability of sustainable urban farming.
  • Streamlined Operations: Operators could manage the complex farm with greater efficiency and fewer errors, reducing operational costs.
  • Scalability & Replication: The well-designed digital infrastructure laid the groundwork for GROWx to scale its operations and replicate the farm model in new locations.
