Human-centric Design

Design is not just about users, but humans whom we need to understand to create useable and engaging experiences. By applying the Design Thinking approach I keep the user as the focal point and my process informed.

User-driven Innovation and co-creation

I have a strong background in facilitation of creative design processes with emphasis on user involvement through participatory and co-design methods. I know that field research must be translated into design strategies in order to create solutions that people will love. People should be involved in design processes, not merely as users of computers, but through holistic engagements as persons with emotions, bodies and thoughts.

Innovation & Concept Development

With a multidisciplinary approach I create concepts through innovation processes and generation of ideas. I have experience with planning and facilitating user-driven processes through workshops and other methods.

User Research

To understand the problem we have to know the user and the design situation. I do this by conducting, fieldstudies, interviews, design workshops and analysis of data. I use these insights to define the problem and to develop personas, scenarios and user journeys.
