Case Study

Digital Strategy (Agentic AI) Energy Optimisation Platform

Pioneering smart EV charging with AI for Shell Recharge.

Overview

Shell Recharge sought to revolutionize the electric vehicle (EV) charging experience by developing an intelligent energy optimization platform. This ambitious project aimed to leverage Agentic AI to provide smart, cost-effective, and sustainable charging solutions for EV owners.

The Problem

EV adoption faces challenges related to charging convenience, cost, and grid strain. Traditional charging methods often lead to higher electricity bills for users and contribute to peak load issues for energy providers. Integrating advanced AI into a user-friendly platform required a sophisticated approach to UX design and system architecture.

The Solution

I played a pivotal role in shaping the Digital Strategy and UX design for the Shell Recharge Energy Optimisation Platform, with a strong focus on embedding Agentic AI capabilities. My contributions included:

  • AI-Driven Service Design & Strategy: Collaborating closely with AI/ML engineers and product teams to define the strategic vision for an agentic AI-powered charging ecosystem. This involved conceptualizing how AI could proactively manage charging schedules based on electricity prices, grid demand, and user preferences.
  • Complex System UX Design: Designing the user experience for a highly sophisticated system where users interact with an intelligent agent. This included creating intuitive interfaces for setting preferences, understanding AI recommendations, and overriding automated decisions, ensuring transparency and user control.
  • User Research & Future Scenario Mapping: Conducting extensive user research to understand EV owner behaviors, pain points, and future expectations regarding smart charging. We used future scenario mapping to anticipate user needs and technological advancements.
  • Prototyping AI Interactions: Developing prototypes to test various interaction models for the agentic AI, ensuring that the system's "intelligence" felt helpful and trustworthy, rather than opaque or intrusive.
  • Information Architecture for Dynamic Data: Structuring the presentation of dynamic energy data, cost savings, and environmental impact information in a clear and understandable manner, enabling users to see the benefits of optimized charging.
  • Cross-functional Collaboration & Product Leadership: Leading cross-functional teams including AI specialists, data scientists, and engineers, fostering a shared understanding of user needs and guiding the development of complex features within an agile framework.

The Impact

The Shell Recharge Energy Optimisation Platform is set to transform the EV charging landscape, offering:

  • Significant Cost Savings for Users: AI-optimized charging schedules allow users to charge during off-peak hours, leading to substantial reductions in electricity costs.
  • Enhanced Grid Stability: The platform contributes to a more balanced energy grid by intelligently distributing charging loads, reducing strain during peak demand.
  • Improved User Experience & Control: Despite the underlying complexity, the intuitive UX empowers users with transparency and control over their smart charging experience.
  • Pioneering AI Integration: Positions Shell Recharge at the forefront of innovation by demonstrating practical and beneficial applications of agentic AI in consumer energy management.
  • Contribution to Sustainability: Promotes the use of renewable energy sources by facilitating charging when green energy is most abundant and affordable.
